Almaty is the largest city in Kazakhstan and its former capital. 
It is known for its beautiful mountains, which provide stunning views and outdoor activities like hiking and skiing.
The city is a cultural and economic hub, with many modern skyscrapers, shopping centers, and historical sites.
Almaty is famous for its apples, especially the Aport variety, which symbolizes the city's name. 
Visitors can explore attractions like Kok-Tobe, Medeu ice rink, and Big Almaty Lake.
